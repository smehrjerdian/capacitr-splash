// THE PROBLEM

Markets and signals are fragmented

Markets exist everywhere — prediction markets, perpetual futures, options — but finding the right market at the right time is still manual, fragmented work.

A trader who follows AI policy news has to manually search Polymarket for relevant prediction markets, check HyperLiquid for NVIDIA perps, and scan Deribit for options plays. The information that drives trading decisions lives in completely different places than the markets themselves.

Nobody connects them. Until now.

// THE INSIGHT

The narrative is the root. Markets are leaves.

Every tradeable market is downstream of a real-world narrative. "Will the Fed cut rates?" is a Polymarket prediction, a BTC perpetual trade, and a set of Treasury options all at once. If you know what someone cares about — and you can see what the world is talking about right now — you can surface exactly the markets that matter to them.

LLM-powered analysis categorizes, summarizes, and extracts tradeable entities. "NVIDIA earnings beat" connects to a perp, an option, and a prediction market about AI chip exports.
